About Us

Construction projects in India has been hugely delaying for multiple reasons. Major part of the blame goes to the executing agencies and material suppliers not being adequately resourceful, reliable, or not having the required quality standards as expected by the builder/client. Hence selection process of vendors and suppliers and closing contract agreements and purchase agreements, in way mostly favourable to the builder/project owner becomes the key aspect of every construction project.

What We Do

We at Gummadi Group, shall take full responsibility of identifying, shortlisting, selecting and appointing various trade contractors and suppliers required for our clients. Our team of engineers who are highly experienced in construction industry, procurement and contract management functions of large projects, would be able to select the most suitable agency, that is most suitable and economical for our clients. We shall handhold the client throughout the process of bidding, tender evaluation, negotiations, untill signing of contract agreements, providing advices safeguarding their interests at every step of the procurement process. All the interactions and communications shall be driven through our online web-portal, which will enable our registered customers (clients/owners, trade contractors and suppliers) to manage the whole process of Tendering, Purchase Quotations till Order Booking, by the press of a button, sitting at their offices, when we do all the background work.
